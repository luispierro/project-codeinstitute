How to Use Survey Insights Today

  1. Upload Your Data:
    • Navigate to our homepage.
    • Click the "Upload & Analyze" button.
    • Select your survey data file in CSV format.
  2. AI Analysis:
    • Our AI engine will automatically process your data.
    • This may take a few moments, depending on the size of your dataset.
  3. View Insights:
    • Once the analysis is complete, you'll see a detailed report, including: Key findings; Visualizations (charts, graphs); and Statistical analysis
  4. Download Your Report:
    • Click the "Download Insights" button to save a comprehensive PDF report of your insights.
    • This report can be shared with your team or clients.
